Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" tab on the login page, fill in your email and a secure password, then press "Sign Up".
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login form to request a reset link.
How can I report a new issue?
After logging in, go to the dashboard and click "Create Issue".
